Dana & Jacob Wedding Review

by Dana Campina

On Saturday, August 23rd, Dana Campina and Jacob Herman were married in the Denver Temple in Colorado. Their entire wedding party and guest stayed at the Denver Temple Inn. The reception was held in the beautiful reception hall at the Inn, complete with decorations and catering. Then after the wedding the guest could stay overnight and enjoy the free breakfast in the morning.

Temple history themed reception and sitting area. A wedding reception hall that can be easily customized with a simple catering menu and kitchen access for food preparation and serving.
