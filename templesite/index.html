The Denver, Colorado Temple

by Damon Harvack

The Denver Temple was the first temple built in Colorado, in 1858. In the beginning the temple had trouble, traffic concerns, neighbors who complained about the lighting of the Temple at night, but eventually thanks to some faithful members of the church the Temple was built and eventually even the neighbors preferred the beauty of the temple lights. The Denver Temple acted like a beacon to the members of the church and more than 28,000 members attended the dedication.
